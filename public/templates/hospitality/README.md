# Grand Stay - Hotels & Residences Template

## Overview
Elegant template designed for hotels, serviced apartments, luxury condominiums, and vacation rentals. Perfect for property management companies, hotel operators, and real estate developers showcasing premium accommodations.

## Concept
This template is specifically designed for businesses offering:
- **Serviced Apartments** - Furnished residences for short and long-term stays
- **Boutique Hotels** - Small to medium-sized hospitality establishments
- **Luxury Condominiums** - High-end residential units for rent or lease
- **Corporate Housing** - Extended stay accommodations for business travelers
- **Vacation Rentals** - Premium holiday accommodations

## Pages Included

### 1. Homepage
- Hero banner with booking CTA
- Featured residences showcase
- Amenities highlights
- Location highlights
- Guest testimonials
- Booking inquiry CTA

### 2. Residences (Room Types)
- All room categories with detailed info
- Studio, 1BR, 2BR, Penthouse suites
- Extended stay packages and pricing
- Room amenities and specifications

### 3. Amenities
- Comprehensive facilities showcase
- Pool, gym, restaurant, business center
- Security and housekeeping services
- Detailed amenity descriptions

### 4. About Us
- Company story and mission
- Why choose us differentiation
- Brand values and commitments

### 5. Contact & Book
- Booking inquiry form
- Special requests handling
- Contact information
- Operating hours

## Key Features

### Widgets Used
- **Hero** - Full-screen banners with booking CTAs
- **Gallery** - Room showcases with pricing
- **Features** - Amenities and benefits grid
- **Testimonials** - Guest reviews and ratings
- **Contact** - Booking inquiry form
- **Text** - Informational content blocks
- **CTA** - Call-to-action sections

### Customization Options
- Room types and pricing editable
- Amenities list customizable
- Extended stay packages configurable
- Contact form fields adjustable
- Images and branding replaceable
